# Will You Be My Valentine?

An interactive and playful Valentine's Day game where you ask someone to be your Valentine! Features animations, a chasing "No" button, and celebration effects.

## Features

- **Interactive Gameplay**: Click "Yes" or try to click "No"
- **Tricky "No" Button**: The "No" button evades your cursor - you can't escape the question!
- **Celebration Screen**: A beautiful celebration screen appears when you say "Yes"
- **Floating Hearts**: Animated hearts floating across the screen
- **Attempt Counter**: Track how many times you tried to click "No"
- **Cute GIF Animations**: Embedded GIFs for personality and charm

## Files

- `index.html` - HTML structure with GIF embeds
- `main.js` - Game logic including button evasion

## How It Works

- The "No" button is programmed to move away when you try to click it
- The "Yes" button takes you to a celebration screen
- An attempt counter shows how determined you are
- Floating hearts animate continuously in the background

## Customization

You can modify:
- The main question text
- The GIF URLs (currently embedded from Giphy)
- Button text and styling
- Celebration message
- Heart animations and colors
- Button evasion speed and distance

## Requirements

- Modern web browser

## Notes

- The "No" button makes it impossible to select "No" - the only way forward is to click "Yes"!
- GIFs are loaded from Giphy, ensure you have internet connection
- Works best on desktop with a mouse (mobile experience may vary)
